Curious case of Target.com

  • January 2006 ...
    The National Federation of the Blind (NFB) sues Target.com for InAccessible website
  • September 2006 ...
    Target wants to dismiss the case saying websites are not convered under American with Disablities Act (ADA)
  • August 2008 ...
    Target Corporation settled class action lawsuit for $6 million and also pays attorney fees of $3,738,864.96

Case of eBay

  • Melissa Earll, a person with deafness wanted to Sell classic comic books, baseball cards on eBay
  • eBay's verification system requires sellers to retrieve and submit a password from a telephone call. She can't hear the password and says eBay doesn't use voice-to-text or other technologies deaf people often rely upon
  • January 2013 ...
    Along with National Association for the Deaf sues eBay

Top 10 Barriers we frequently see at PayPal

  1. Screen reader says edit form field blank (Labels)
  2. Links don't take be anywhere, doesn't work (empty links)
  3. Buttons are not keyboard navigable (fake elements)
  4. Screen reader says blank when a dialog is open
  5. Keyboard trap: can't edit with screen reader
  6. Errors are not friendly
  7. No visual focus indicator
  8. Poor color contrast
